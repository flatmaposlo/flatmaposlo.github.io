Call for Speakers share your knowledge

Have a story to tell? Come share your knowledge and experience with functional programming. flatMap(Oslo) 2014 will include 40 minute talks and 2 hour workhops. We hope you want to join us in making flatMap(Oslo) 2014 an experience out of the ordinary.

flatMap's main focus is on Scala and other functional programming languages on the Java Virtual Machine. However, this shouldn't keep you from suggesting "something different."

We hope we get to see some suggestions for:

  • experience reports
  • introductory and advanced technical topics
  • 2 hour workshops
  • BoF (informal discussion groups)
  • something weird, offbeat, eccentric, fun, interesting, ...
