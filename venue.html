Venue Norsk Design- og Arkitektursenter

flatMap(Oslo) 2014 will be held at DogA - the Norwegian Centre for Design and Architecture. This refurbished old transformer station is a meeting place for design, architecture and related subjects.

DogA - is situated in Hausmanns gate 16 beside the Akerselva river, not far from Grünerløkka in Oslo.
